VisionMedia Digital Signage (by Zenesis Limited)
The VisionMedia Digital Signage platform has over 1,500 set top box installations, each of which connect to the Qooxdoo-based web services to download media and instructions. The Qooxdoo based control panel administers the content and facilities for each site, and the owners of each site (of which there are over 4,000 users) log in via a seperate Qooxdoo based members application to customise the content broadcast to their screen.
